See the Platform

VERA and COMPLY — already live in all fifty states.

The case in these pages is the argument. The platform is the instrument the argument calls for, and it is already built, deployed, and running — in all fifty states and DC, plus Ontario, the Netherlands, Australia, New Zealand, and Tokyo.

Both sides shipped: VERA for Type 4 detection, LCAP verification, teacher observation, and MCP query; and VERA Comply, the SB 1288 six-module product for California districts. The infrastructure exists; the question is will.
